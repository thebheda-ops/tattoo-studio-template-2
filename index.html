Questions?

Everything you need to know before your first sit.

Does it hurt?

Pain is subjective, but most clients find it manageable. We describe it as a 'hot scratch' or mild irritation.

How do I prepare?

Eat a full meal, stay hydrated, and wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the area being tattooed.

Pricing?

We charge by design or by session. Minimum starting price is NPR 3,000 for small custom work.
